Founded by two brothers with a passion for functional design and timeless style, Smith Brothers Remodeling has been transforming homes in the Seattle-Tacoma area since 2020. What started as a love for quality craftsmanship in kitchen and bathroom remodels has grown into a trusted, full-service renovation business rooted in comfort, luxury, and results that last.

Our Mission: Disrupt the Ordinary

At Smith Brothers, we don’t just renovate — we disrupt. Our mission is to raise the bar for home remodeling with exceptional customer service, transparent communication, free estimates, and a seamless project experience from start to finish. You’ll be posting your “after” photos in no time.

What Sets Us Apart

We’re proud to be locally owned and operated, serving homeowners across the Seattle-Tacoma metro area. Every remodel is handled with precision and care — because your home deserves excellence. We are licensed, insured, and bonded to protect your investment every step of the way.
